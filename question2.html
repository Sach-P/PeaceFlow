Are you experiencing anger, emptiness, loss of interest and/or reduced concentration?


Depression causes feelings of sadness and/or a loss of interest in activities you once enjoyed. It can lead to a variety of emotional and physical problems and can decrease your ability to function at work and at home.


Symptoms may include: irritability, anxiousness, loss of appetite, and feeling tired easily.


Treatments may include: Medication such as antidepressants, psychotherapy, self help through things such as regular exercise and good quality sleep.


Please ask a qualified healthcare provider if you are experiencing any of these symptoms.
