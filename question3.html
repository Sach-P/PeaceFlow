Are you experiencing difficulty concentrating, fatigue and exhaustion, sweaty palms, and/or shaking?


Generalized anxiety disorder involves persistent and excessive worry that interferes with daily activities.


Symptoms may include: irritability, muscle tension, and difficulty sleeping.


Treatments may include: Psychotherapy, medications (to allieviate symptoms), joining support groups, and lowering caffiene intake.


Please ask a qualified healthcare provider if you are experiencing any of these symptoms.
