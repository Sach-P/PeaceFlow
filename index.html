The purpose of our website is to assist users with their mental health. If the user is feeling stressed, moody, or anxious, the site will monitor the status of their condition with surveys and journal entries. This information would be stored for long-term purposes to be reviewed later. On top of that, the survey determines the conditons for their current symptoms. The site has meditation guidance that will calm the user with a breathing technique. If the user utilizes the site occasionally, they may become mentally stronger.
